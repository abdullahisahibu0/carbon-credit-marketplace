# Carbon Credit Marketplace

A transparent carbon offset trading platform built on blockchain technology that enables verified environmental impact tracking and secure carbon credit transactions.

## Overview

This platform revolutionizes the carbon offset market by providing complete transparency and verification in carbon credit trading. Similar to Toucan Protocol, our system tokenizes carbon credits on blockchain, allowing organizations like companies and environmental DAOs to purchase and retire carbon offsets transparently, with real-time tracking of environmental impact.

## Key Features

### Carbon Credit Tokenization
- Convert verified carbon credits into blockchain tokens
- Immutable record of carbon credit provenance and verification
- Fractional ownership enabling micro-investments in environmental projects
- Standardized token format for cross-platform compatibility

### Transparent Trading Platform
- Decentralized marketplace for buying and selling carbon credits
- Real-time price discovery through automated market making
- Transparent order books and trading history
- Reduced intermediary costs and fees

### Environmental Impact Tracking
- Real-time tracking of environmental benefits and outcomes
- Integration with satellite imagery and IoT sensors
- Verification of actual carbon sequestration and reduction
- Detailed impact reporting for stakeholders

### Verified Project Registry
- Comprehensive database of verified carbon offset projects
- Due diligence records and certification tracking
- Project performance monitoring and reporting
- Quality assurance and fraud prevention

## Smart Contracts

### Carbon Tracker Contract
The core contract that handles:
- **Credit Tokenization**: Convert verified carbon credits into tradeable tokens
- **Trading Facilitation**: Enable secure peer-to-peer and institutional trading
- **Impact Metrics**: Track and verify environmental impact data
- **Double-Spending Prevention**: Ensure credits cannot be sold multiple times
- **Retirement Mechanism**: Permanent removal of credits from circulation

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites
- Clarinet CLI installed
- Node.js for testing framework
- Access to Stacks blockchain

### Installation
1. Clone the repository
2. Install dependencies: `npm install`
3. Run tests: `clarinet test`
4. Check contracts: `clarinet check`

### Development Workflow
1. Develop contracts in the `contracts/` directory
2. Write tests in the `tests/` directory
3. Use `clarinet check` to validate syntax
4. Deploy to testnet for integration testing
